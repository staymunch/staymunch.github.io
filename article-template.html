Best Cold Air Intakes for the 2023 VW Jetta GLI Autobahn

The GLI's 2.0T is a solid engine from the factory but the stock airbox is conservative. Swapping it out is one of the best first mods you can do because it makes the car louder, punchier and more responsive without touching the tune.

The 2023 Jetta GLI Autobahn shares its turbocharged 2.0-liter engine architecture with the Golf GTI, though it produces 228 horsepower compared to the GTI's 241. The difference in power output comes partly from software and partly from the intake setup. A better intake lets the turbo breathe more freely, which translates to improved spool time and that satisfying induction sound at wide-open throttle.

What to look for in a GLI intake

Not every cold air intake is created equal for the A7 Jetta platform. The engine bay layout means you need fitment confirmed specifically for the GLI, not just the GTI. The two are related but not interchangeable when it comes to intake routing. Look for a kit that includes a heat shield, uses a high-flow cone filter and comes with all required hardware for a clean install.

After running the CTS Turbo intake on our white 2023 GLI Autobahn for several months, the most noticeable change was at partial throttle in Sport mode. The turbo spools faster and the engine pulls more eagerly from 3,000 RPM upward. Paired with a COBB Accessport Stage 1 93-octane map the combination is genuinely satisfying for a daily driver.

Runner Up: IE Intake

Integrated Engineering makes a strong alternative that some owners prefer for its more subtle sound profile. If you want the airflow gains without as much intake noise in the cabin, the IE kit is worth considering. It sits slightly higher in price but comes with excellent build quality and a lifetime filter warranty.

Final verdict

Both intakes are solid choices for the 2023 Jetta GLI Autobahn. If you want more driver engagement and a sportier sound, go with CTS Turbo. If you prefer a cleaner, quieter install with a lifetime warranty, go with IE. Either way, this is one of the best value-per-dollar mods available for the A7 GLI platform and a natural first step before adding a tune.
