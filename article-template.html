Best Cold Air Intakes
for the 2023 VW Jetta GLI Autobahn

The GLI's EA888 2.0T is well-engineered from the factory, but the stock airbox is deliberately conservative. A cold air intake is one of the best first mods you can do — it makes the car louder, punchier, and more responsive without touching the tune.

The A7 Jetta GLI Autobahn produces 228 horsepower from the same EA888 engine family as the Golf GTI. The GTI makes 241hp partly because of software and partly because of a freer intake path. A better intake lets the turbo spool faster and extends the powerband noticeably in the mid-range.

What to look for

Fitment confirmation specifically for the Jetta GLI is critical — not just "MQB." The A7 Jetta engine bay routes differently from the Golf. Look for a sealed heat shield, a high-flow cone filter, and confirmed A7 GLI compatibility in the product listing.
