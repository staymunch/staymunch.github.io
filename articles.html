Best Cold Air Intakes
for the 2023 VW Jetta GLI Autobahn

The GLI's EA888 2.0T is well-engineered from the factory, but the stock airbox is deliberately conservative. A cold air intake is one of the best first mods you can do — no tune needed, immediate improvement.

The A7 Jetta GLI Autobahn produces 228 horsepower from the EA888 engine family. A better intake lets the turbo breathe more freely, improving spool time and giving you the induction sound the stock airbox completely muffles.

What to look for

Confirm fitment specifically for the Jetta GLI, not just "MQB." The A7 Jetta routes differently from the Golf. Look for a sealed heat shield, high-flow cone filter, and confirmed A7 GLI compatibility in the listing.
