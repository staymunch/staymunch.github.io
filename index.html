About this site

The GLI deserves better coverage.

Most automotive sites treat the Jetta GLI as a footnote to the Golf GTI. We disagree. The A7 GLI is more practical, comes with the same limited-slip differential, runs the same EA888 engine family, and costs significantly less. It is an enthusiast's car hiding in a commuter's body.

Every guide on this site comes from a 2023 GLI Autobahn Opal White with the Black Package — running Stage 1 on 93 octane, CTS Turbo intake, turbo inlet pipe, and a few other mods we'll cover in detail.
