Written by an actual owner

This site covers the 2023 Jetta GLI Autobahn from the perspective of someone who actually owns one. White with the Black Package, 228 horsepower, 7-speed DSG. Every mod mentioned here has either been tested personally or thoroughly researched in the GLI community. No filler. No generic listicles.

The GLI sits in a unique spot. More practical than the Golf GTI, more fun than the standard Jetta, and punching well above its price point with the limited-slip differential and adaptive suspension. It deserves better content than what is out there.
