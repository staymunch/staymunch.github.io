About GLI Garage

GLI Garage is dedicated to providing honest, in depth reviews of performance modifications for the Volkswagen GLI. We're not here to sell you parts or push affiliate links. We're enthusiasts who buy, install, and test the mods ourselves so you can make informed decisions about your build.

Every modification we review goes through extensive real world testing, including dyno runs, data logging, and thousands of miles of driving. We cover everything from budget friendly upgrades to high end performance parts, always with transparency about costs, installation difficulty, and actual results.

Whether you're running a bone stock daily driver or a fully built track weapon, GLI Garage is here to help you choose the right modifications for your goals and budget.

Editorial Disclosure

GLI Garage maintains editorial independence in all product reviews and recommendations. We purchase the majority of products we test with our own funds. In cases where manufacturers provide products for review, this is clearly disclosed in the article. We are not paid for positive reviews, and all opinions expressed are our own based on thorough testing and real world use.

Some links on this site may be affiliate links, which means we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase through them, at no additional cost to you. These commissions help support the site and allow us to continue providing detailed, unbiased reviews. Our affiliate relationships never influence our editorial content or recommendations.
