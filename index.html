About GLI Garage

GLI Garage is dedicated to providing honest, data-driven reviews of aftermarket modifications for the Volkswagen Jetta GLI. Every part we review is purchased, installed, and tested on our own 2023 GLI Autobahn in Opal White with Black Package. We use dyno testing, real world performance metrics, and long term durability assessments to give you the information you need to make smart modification decisions. No sponsored fluff, just real results from real enthusiasts.
